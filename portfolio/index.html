Game Modification: The Dice Game: ADD & CALL
The Dice Game: ADD & CALL modifies the traditional dice game where randomness dominates by adding two new features, ADD and CALL, to allow the game be enjoyed by a team with a little strategy and some friendly competetion.

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: The BoD
The BoD is a highly flexible strategy board game that simulates a simplified yet competitive market, where players act as board of directors and lead their companies to success. The game requires players to make business judgements, take market risks, and retain sustainable growth, while aiming to achieve absolute dominance of their own company or the market. Players can win the game through a variety of approaches which include but are not limited to cooperative competitive, and even hostile approaches.
